William E. Olsen

I currently hold a post doctoral position in the mathematics department at the University of Virginia.

My research centers on the smooth topology of low-dimensional manifolds. I use tools such as:

  • Trisections of four-manifolds and embedded surfaces
  • Heegaard Floer homology
  • Microlocal sheaf theory and its connections with Fukaya categories of cotangent bundles.
  • Legendrian contact homology
  • Quilted Floer cohomology
